The Studio

WHERE SCIENCE
MEETS
COUTURE

Founded in Milan in 2001 by textile engineer Dr. Leena Varma and fashion designer Marco Alteri, Veldra was built on a single conviction: luxury and performance need not be opposites.

Our fabrics are worn on runways in Paris, tested in the laboratories of elite sportswear houses, and used in aerospace-grade interior applications. The thread count is the same — the intent is everything.

Our Technology

ENGINEERED
AT THE THREAD

01

Nano-Surface Coating

Proprietary NanoShield™ process bonds water, stain, and UV repellency at the molecular level without altering hand-feel or breathability.

02

Hybrid Yarn Architecture

Custom core-spun yarns combining natural luxury fibres with high-tenacity technical filaments — single material, dual performance.

03

Phase-Change Thermal Regulation

Microencapsulated PCM technology woven into fabric structure, not applied as a coating — permanent, washfast, and invisible.

04

AI-Assisted Dobby Design

Our Milan R&D studio uses generative weave-structure algorithms to prototype constructions 80% faster than traditional methods.

05

Zero Liquid Discharge Dyeing

Closed-loop supercritical CO₂ dyeing on select lines — no water used, no effluent produced. Colour intensity matches conventional wet processes.

Process

PHASE 01
Fibre Selection & Sourcing
Every fibre origin is audited. We work directly with Mongolian cashmere co-ops, Italian silk throwsters, and certified recycled polyester suppliers. No intermediaries. No compromise.
PHASE 02
Yarn Engineering
Our yarn lab in Surat blends, twists, and core-spins custom constructions. Each yarn recipe is proprietary to the client's brief and protected under NDA.
PHASE 03
Weaving & Knitting
Multi-rapier jacquard looms and flatbed knitting systems rated up to 200 inches. CAD-to-loom in 48 hours for sampling.
PHASE 04
Technical Finishing
NanoShield™ coating, phase-change impregnation, enzyme bio-finishing, calendering, and Sanfor-stretch — applied with zero cross-contamination between runs.
PHASE 05
QC, Certification & Dispatch
Digital fabric inspection at 120m/min. Bluesign®, OEKO-TEX®, and Cradle to Cradle reporting available. White-glove delivery to studio.
